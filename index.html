Game Rules:

Each player rolls the dice (ROLL DICE button) scoring points for each turn until one of the dice falls 1 or the player decides to stop (HOLD button) If one is dropped, the player does not receive anything for this round. If a player decides to stop before throwing 1, all points earned per round are added to his total score. Then the turn goes to another player. The winner is the one who is the first to score 100 points. To start a new game, press NEW GAME
